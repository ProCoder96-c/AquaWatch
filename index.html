Causes

  • Sewage and wastewater

  • Oil spills

Effects

  • Harm to aquatic life
  • Waterborne diseases
  • Disruption of ecosystems
  • Contaminated drinking water
  • Economic impacts

Prevention

  • Proper waste treatment
  • Reduce plastic use
  • Sustainable agriculture
  • Community monitoring
  • Policy enforcement
